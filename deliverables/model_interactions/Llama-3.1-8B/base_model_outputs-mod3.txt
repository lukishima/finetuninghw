Example:

- 1 cup spaghetti pasta
- 1/2 tablespoon garlic, minced
- 2 tablespoons olive oil
- 1/4 teaspoon salt

Steps:
1. Bring a pot of water to a boil over medium-high heat.
2. Add salt to the water, then add the pasta. Cook until al dente according to package instructions.
3. Meanwhile, heat olive oil in a pan and sauté the minced garlic until fragrant.
