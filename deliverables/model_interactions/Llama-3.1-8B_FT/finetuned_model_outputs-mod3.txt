- tomato
- basil
- mozzarella cheese
- olive oil
- salt
- pepper

Steps:

2. Slice the tomatoes in half and place them cut side up on a baking sheet. Sprinkle with salt and pepper.

4. Bake for 15-20 minutes, until the tomatoes are softened and the basil is wilted.
5. Top with mozzarella cheese and return to the oven for another 5-10 minutes, until the cheese is melted and bubbly.
6. Serve immediately."]
