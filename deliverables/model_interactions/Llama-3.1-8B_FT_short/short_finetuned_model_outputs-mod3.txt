Ingredients:
- salmon
- lemon
- garlic
- fresh dill
- olive oil
- salt
- pepper

Steps:
1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees F. Line a baking sheet with foil and grease with olive oil.
2. In a small bowl, combine the garlic, salt, and pepper. Add the salmon and toss to coat. Place the salmon on the baking sheet.
3. Bake for 10-12 minutes, or until the salmon is cooked through. Remove from the oven and let cool slightly. Top with fresh dill and lemon wedges. Serve immediately.
